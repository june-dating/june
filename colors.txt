Dominant Colors:
Violet / Purple Gradient Background

Starts with a deep violet (#5E2CA5 or similar)

Fades into a lighter lavender/pastel purple (#B79AFB to #D4C2FF range)

Dark Modal Container

Likely a very dark charcoal black or deep gray (#1A1A1A or #121212)

White / Light Text

Used for contrast on the dark background, typically #FFFFFF or #F8F8F8

Soft Gray for Placeholder and Secondary Text

Subtle muted gray for input hints and descriptions, around #AAAAAA or #888888
